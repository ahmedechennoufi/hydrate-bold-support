How do I set up sleep schedule?
During onboarding, you'll set your bedtime and wake-up time. This ensures you won't receive hydration reminders while sleeping.
Can I track beverages other than water?
Yes! Track water, coffee, tea, juice, soft drinks, sports drinks, milk, and other beverages. Each category has specific drink types to choose from.
How do I edit or delete entries?
In Today's Records or History screen, tap the delete button (üóëÔ∏è) next to any entry to remove it. Confirm deletion when prompted.
Can I change my daily water goal?
Yes, you can customize your daily hydration goal in the app settings based on your personal needs and activity level.
Why am I not receiving reminders?
Check that notifications are enabled in your phone's Settings > Notifications > Hydrate Bold. Also ensure you're not in your set sleep hours.
How do I view my hydration history?
Tap the History tab to see your past intake organized by date, plus statistics showing your hydration patterns over time.

App crashes or freezes
Try closing and reopening the app. If issues persist, restart your device or reinstall the app. Your data is saved locally.
Reminders not working
Ensure notifications are enabled in Settings. Check that you're not in Do Not Disturb mode or within your sleep schedule hours.

Make sure you have sufficient storage space on your device. Close other apps if memory is low.
